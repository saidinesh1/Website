Aim of Joining Defence :

Most of our actions are influenced by the path we choose in life,it may be influenced by sports,studies,dance,music,friends,food,hobbies and habits. But my life after completing school is influenced by a unique passion which most of us are not awared about it.yes,I have a passion of joining one of the elite security forces in this combat world, "Indian Army."
